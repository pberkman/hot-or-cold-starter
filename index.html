Directions

  • 1. The game will choose a random number between 1 and 100.
  • 2. Your job is to guess until you can guess the hidden secret number.
  • 3. After each guess you will get clues as to if you are getting hotter or colder.
  • 4. Choose your guesses wisely...at the end of the game your total number of guesses is the number of burpees you have to do.

Good Luck!
